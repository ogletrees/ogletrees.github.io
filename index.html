scott ogletree

In the PhD program at Clemson University - Department of Parks, Recreation, and Tourism Management. My research interest is in the role of greenspace in urban areas.

Other things I'm interested in include geographic information systems (GIS), design, maps & cartography, statistics, visualization, and bikes.

Also currently working as Database Specialist in the Department of Planning, Development and Preservation
